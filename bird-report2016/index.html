18/3/16 Bird Report




Birds in order in which sighted on this year's Twitchathon. This is an annual walk to and from Fingrinhoe Wick reserve. The weather was cold and overcast with periods of drizzle and a northerly breeze. The tide was, once again, too low at Fingringhoe, meaning that most waders were distant silhouettes. I must learn.
