Model Making


American AAFV and Toyota Pick-Up. 1/50th scale.

Toys for grown-up(sic) boys. For war gamers to be precise. Here we have an amphibious American armoured troop carrier and a Toyota pick-up for the Taliban. Asymetric warfare anyone?

Russian BTR80 troop carrier. 1/50th scale.

I made these prototypes for Imprint Models. They are then cast and sold to War Gamers around the globe. Their requirements are for models of accurate scale and appearance but of few parts and, thus, of simple construction.

Leopard2_A6. 1/50th scale.

Imprint Models are no more. Their lines have been taken over by Empress Miniatures. This is the first of my models for them.

Lee-Enfield Rifle.

This was not for war gamers. This model is 1/4 scale to accompany an 18inch First War soldier being sculpted by Jill Desborough.
