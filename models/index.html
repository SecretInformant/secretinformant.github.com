Model Making

American AAFV and Toyota Pick-Up. 1/50th scale.

Toys for grown-up(sic) boys. For war gamers to be precise.
Here we have an amphibious American armoured troop carrier alongside a Toyota pick-up for the Taliban.
Asymmetric warfare anyone?

Russian BTR80 troop carrier. 1/50th scale.

I made these prototypes for Imprint Models. They are then cast and sold to War Gamers around the globe.
Their requirements are for models of accurate scale and appearance but of few parts and, thus, of simple construction.

Leopard2_A6. 1/50th scale.

This is my only model for them.
