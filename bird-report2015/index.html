17/3/15 Bird Report




Birds in order in which sighted on this year's Twitchathon. This is a reprise of the annual walk to and from Fingrinhoe Wick reserve. The weather was dry with periods of watery sunshine. A bit too misty for good viewing. The tide was low at Fingringhoe meaning that many waders were distant silhouettes.
